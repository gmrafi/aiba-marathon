About the Event

Justice Half Marathon 2025 is an on-ground event only, bringing together runners from all over Bangladesh. Enjoy a scenic run, professional organization, and a host of facilities and rewards!

Event Categories

  • 21 KM Half Marathon (Open for All)
    • 400 participants
    • Registration Fee: BDT 1050
    • Start & End: Army IBA Sylhet (via Gotatikor Point)
    • Male & Female Podium Rewards: Top 3 get prize money + crest, 4th & 5th get crest only
  • 10 KM Long Run (Open for All, including 60+)
    • 800 participants
    • Registration Fee: BDT 850
    • Start: Gotatikor Point, End: Army IBA Sylhet
    • Elite & Professionals not encouraged
    • Male, Female & Senior Citizen Rewards: Top 3 get prize money + crest, 4th & 5th get crest only

🎁 All Registered Participants Receive

  • Official T-shirt
  • Participant Medal
  • Digital Certificate
  • Backpack
  • Gifts (optional)

✅ Facilities

  • Bus service from town to track
  • Hydration & Food Support
  • Medical Facilities on Track
  • Warm-up & Cool-down Zone Access
  • Dressing Room & Washroom facilities

Note: Virtual participation is not allowed.
