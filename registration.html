Justice Half Marathon 2025

Organized by the Army IBA Hiking & Trekking Club, this is the first-ever national marathon hosted by our institute and we’re inviting runners from all over the country to be a part of something meaningful, energetic, and unforgettable.

Whether you're a seasoned runner, a student, or just looking to have fun — there's a category for everyone!

Event Categories

21 KM Half Marathon Tk. 1250 For the experienced runners ready to go the distance.
10 KM Long Run Tk. 1050 A great choice for passionate runners looking for a solid challenge.
10 KM Student Run Tk. 800 Specially designed for students who want to compete and enjoy the spirit of the run.
Kids' 1 KM Fun Run Tk. 600 A short and exciting run just for the little champions.
