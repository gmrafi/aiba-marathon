Common Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

Response times vary by priority: High (4-6 hours), Medium (24 hours), Low (2-3 business days).

Can I track my query status?

You'll receive email updates as your query is processed. For urgent matters, contact us directly via WhatsApp.

What if I need immediate assistance?

For urgent issues, use our WhatsApp support or call during office hours (9 AM - 6 PM daily).

Are there any charges for support?

All support services are completely free for registered participants and general inquiries.
